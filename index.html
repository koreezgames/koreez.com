PARALLYZED

Parallyzed is an atmospheric adventure platformer with unique gameplay, set in a dark and enchanting dreamscape.

You play both sisters simultaneously. Red and Blue have the ability to swap bodies at any time. Help them make their way through an ever-difficult, spooky landscape that keeps trying to block their progress. Each sister's color and size must overcome the color and size of each dimension's obstacles. Split your mind in two and get lost in the parallel beauty of Parallyzed!

  • Released: September 2016
  • Platforms: iOS , Android, Steam,

ROOFBOT

Roofbot’s toylike world oozes playful charm. Carrying colored shapes to matching holes? Sounds like a job for a cute robot.

Guide Roofie through gorgeous, meditative rooftops and try to get the right color energy balls into the proper slots. But beware, the architecture changes as you interact with it so think ahead - every step you take may lead you closer to the goal, or trap you on the roof forever. Explore gorgeous audio and fantastic, hand-crafted architecture while trying to figure out ever-more-cunning puzzles.

  • Released: October 2016
  • Platforms: iOS , Android, Steam,

ADVENTURES OF LITERATUS

Educational game teaching media literacy.

The story goes that one day the princess of Informia land who is the guarantor of truth and trust disappears. Rumors spread that she is marrying Manipulus - the oppressor of the neighboring land who always wanted to occupy Informia. But Literatus, Veritas’s lover doesn’t believe the lies and decides to find his lover. On his way to find and free Veritas Literatus visits 10 destinations and on every stage of the game he needs to verify information, debunk fakes and find out the truth to overcome the challenges.

PLAY
  • Client: Media Initiatives Center and USAID
